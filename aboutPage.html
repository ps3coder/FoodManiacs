ABOUT US

The Food Maniacs Co. stands apart from other brands for its crazily crafted recipes. Known for its signature twin sauce combos for most of its recipes. The Food Maniacs Co. stands apart for its use of the freshest ingredients and all orders prepared hot and fresh to order. The story of its inception began when two friends, after brainstorming during the 2020 COVID lockdown and hustling through the challenges of work and business post-lockdown, decided to put their past work experience to a real-world test and start their own food business. With limited access to funds and a restricted market scenario due to COVID, they started selling momos on the streets. On seeing a very good response from the market, they decided to upscale their business by renting a shop and thus created the brand "The Food Maniacs Co." Having built their market presence for the last 4 years and having opened 3 company outlets in Ahmedabad, they are now ready to take their brand and business to the next level.
